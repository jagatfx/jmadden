Startup Weekend Bali

22 Nov 2015 on Startup, Bali, Design-my-day

I attended and pitched one of my ideas at Startup Weekend Bali 2015 in Ubud, Bali, held at the coworking space there hubud. My idea, Design My Day, and pitch were well-received and I was able to gather an extraordinary team. We worked very well together for the weekend and came up with a powerful pitch that ended up being awarded the winning startup for the weekend. Now it is time to build out our concept and see if the world embraces it as something helpful.
